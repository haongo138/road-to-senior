# Behavioral

The behavioral round tests judgment, collaboration, and ownership through your past
experience. Structure every answer with **STAR-L**:

> **S**ituation · **T**ask · **A**ction · **R**esult · **L**earned

The **Learned** beat — what you took away and now do differently — is what separates a
senior answer from a war story. See the [STAR-L Method](./star-method) for the full
framework and delivery tips.

Each question below is a **worksheet**: what the interviewer is really assessing,
prompts to fill in your own STAR-L story, common red flags, and a collapsed example to
compare against. Prepare 6–8 real stories from your experience — most questions are
variations you can map onto a story you've already written.
